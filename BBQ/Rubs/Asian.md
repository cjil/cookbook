# Asian inspired BBQ rub

## Recipe Details
- Servings: Per kg of meat
- Difficulty: Easy
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cook time: N/A
- Tags: barbecue, rub, Asian-inspired
- Categories: Rubs, BBQ

## Preamble

This rub blends bold barbecue seasoning with Asian-inspired aromatics, giving grilled meat a richer, slightly spicy flavour profile that complements pork, beef, or chicken.

## Ingredients

Measurements are based on 1 kg of meat
- Pork:
  - 2 tsp coarse sea salt
  - 1 tbsp Chinese five spice
  - 1 tbsp garlic powder
  - 2 tsp ginger powder
  - 1.5 tsp ground white pepper
  - 0.5 tsp ground Szechuan pepper
  - 1.5 tbsp brown sugar
- Beef:
  - 1 tsp coarse sea salt
  - 2 tsp Chinese five spice
  - 1 tbsp garlic powder
  - 1 tsp ginger powder
  - 1 tsp ground white pepper
  - 0.5 tsp ground Szechuan pepper
  - 1 tbsp black pepper

## Method

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix until evenly blended.
2. Rub the mixture over the surface of the meat.
3. Let the seasoned meat rest in the refrigerator for 12–24 hours before cooking.
