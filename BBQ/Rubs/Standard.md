# Standard BBQ rub

## Recipe Details
- Servings: Per kg of meat
- Difficulty: Easy
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cook time: N/A
- Tags: barbecue, rub, classic
- Categories: Rubs, BBQ

## Preamble

A classic American-style BBQ rub with simple pantry spices, built to enhance the natural smoky flavour of brisket, pork ribs, or chicken without overpowering the meat.

## Ingredients

Measurements are based on 1 kg of meat
- 3/4 tsp coarse sea salt
- 1 tbsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp dry mustard powder
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika

## Method

1. Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and stir until thoroughly blended.
2. Pat the meat dry, then evenly coat it with the rub.
3. Let the meat rest for at least 1 hour, or refrigerate for up to 12 hours, before cooking.
4. For pork, add 1–2 tbsp brown sugar to the rub, or brush on a liquid sweetener (such as maple syrup or honey) when wrapping, to enhance caramelization and flavour.
