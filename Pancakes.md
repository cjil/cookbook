# Pancakes

## Recipe Details
- Servings: 1
- Difficulty: Easy
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cook time: 10 minutes
- Tags: breakfast, quick, family
- Categories: Breakfast

## Preamble
I found this recipe on YouTube a few years ago, and it is the easiest most consistent recipe I have ever used for making pancakes.

## Ingredients
- 1 egg
- plain flour (same weight as the egg)
- 1 tsp baking powder
- milk (same weight as the egg, or 1.5–2× egg weight for thinner pancakes)
- 1 pinch of salt

## Method
1. Weigh the egg.
2. In a large mixing bowl, weigh out the same amount of flour as the egg weight.
3. Add the baking powder and salt to the flour.
4. Separate the egg, placing the yolk into a jug and the white into a second mixing bowl.
5. Weigh out the milk and add it to the jug with the egg yolk. For thicker pancakes, use equal weight of milk to egg; for thinner pancakes, increase the milk to 1.5–2 times the egg weight.
6. Using a balloon whisk, beat the egg whites until fluffy.
7. Mix the milk and egg yolk mixture into the flour mixture.
8. Fold the egg whites into the batter.
9. Cook over a low-medium heat in a buttered frypan.

This will make thick fluffy pancakes. If you prefer them thinner, increase the milk so the egg-to-milk weight ratio is about 1:1.5 or 1:2.
